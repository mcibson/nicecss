The 3 or 10 Rule

  • Maximum 3 .classes per <HTML Element>
  • Maximum 10 @extends per Class
  • Maximum 3 @extends per <HTML Elementlement>
  • Maximum 10 Characters per BEM Entity max9Chars__max9Chars--max9Chars
  • Maximum 3 Entities in a BEM Selector;
